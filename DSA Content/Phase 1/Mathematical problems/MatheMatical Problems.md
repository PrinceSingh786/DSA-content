# 🧮 Mathematics for Programming

A structured roadmap to build mathematical problem-solving skills from **beginner** to **advanced**.

---

# 🌱 Level 1 – Basic Number Operations

> Learn how to manipulate digits of a number.

- ✅ Even or Odd
- ✅ Positive, Negative, or Zero
- ✅ Count Digits
- ✅ Sum of Digits
- ✅ Product of Digits
- ✅ Reverse a Number
- ✅ Check if a Number is Palindrome
- ✅ Find Maximum Digit
- ✅ Find Minimum Digit
- ✅ Replace all `0`s with `1`s

---

# ➕ Level 2 – Basic Mathematics

> Build the foundation of mathematical programming.

- ✅ Sum of First N Natural Numbers
- ✅ Power of a Number
- ✅ Factorial of a Number
- ✅ Print Fibonacci Series up to N Terms

        int b = 1;

- ✅ Greatest of Two Numbers
- ✅ Greatest of Three Numbers
- ✅ Check Leap Year
- ✅ Calculate Area of a Circle
- ✅ Find Roots of a Quadratic Equation

---

# 🔢 Level 3 – Factors & Prime Numbers

> Learn divisibility, factors, and prime numbers.

- ✅ Find All Factors of a Number
- ✅ Count Number of Factors
- ✅ Check if a Number is Prime
- ✅ Print Prime Numbers in a Given Range 
- ✅ Print Prime Factors of a Number

---

# ⚡ Level 4 – GCD & LCM

> Learn efficient number theory techniques.

- ✅ Find GCD (Brute Force)
- ✅ Find GCD using Euclid's Algorithm
- ✅ Find LCM of Two Numbers

---

# ⭐ Level 5 – Special Number Problems

> Practice famous mathematical properties.

- ✅ Armstrong Number
- ✅ Perfect Number   - Sum of proper divisors
- ✅ Strong Number   - Sum of digit factorials
- ✅ Automorphic Number  - Square ends in the number itself 
- ✅ Harshad (Niven) Number  - divisible by the sum of its own digits
- ✅ Abundant Number

---

# 📈 Level 6 – Range-Based Problems

> Extend previous concepts to multiple numbers.

- ✅ Print Palindrome Numbers in a Range
- ✅ Print Armstrong Numbers in a Range
- ✅ Print Perfect Numbers in a Range
- ✅ Print Strong Numbers in a Range

---

# 📊 Level 7 – Series Problems

> Learn mathematical series and summations.

- ✅ Sum of an Arithmetic Progression (AP)
- ✅ Sum of a Geometric Progression (GP)
- ✅ Sum of Numbers in a Given Range

---

# 💻 Level 8 – Number System Conversions

> Understand different number representations.

- ✅ Binary → Decimal
- ✅ Decimal → Binary
- ✅ Binary → Octal
- ✅ Octal → Binary
- ✅ Decimal → Octal
- ✅ Octal → Decimal

---

# 🚀 Level 9 – Mathematical Applications

> Apply mathematics to real programming problems.

- ✅ Add Two Fractions
- ✅ Check Whether a Number Can Be Expressed as the Sum of Two Prime Numbers
- ✅ Permutations (N People Occupying R Seats)
- ✅ Convert Number to Words
